॥ शिवोऽहम् ॥

Compiled, in seva, over six years.

The questions in this archive were addressed to Guruji over the course of six years, in private correspondence and in the small Telegram sangha he kept. They were not written for publication; what is gathered here are the answers worth keeping — the ones his students returned to more than once when their own practice grew thin.

The Sanskrit is set in देवनागरी alongside transliteration, so a reader unfamiliar with the script may still hear the syllables as they are meant to sound. Where Guruji used a phrase whose ordinary translation would dilute it, the original has been kept and a note placed beside it.

"The questions are not mine. The answers are not mine. What is mine is the silence between them — and even that, I borrowed."

The codex is offered freely, in the manner of anugraha — grace passed along, not held. New entries are added as they arise. Older entries are revised, gently, when a wiser reading appears.

ॐ — compiled by the students of the Sangha, 2026.
